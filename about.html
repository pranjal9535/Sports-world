ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION

Games and sports mean a lot in people’s life whether to make a bright career or maintain the fitness level all through the life. It is multi-beneficial for the players in various dimensions of the life. If one is interested in this field, he/she can get success in every walk of life. Sports and games can be outdoor, indoor and all the gymnastic activities by the athletics. Children involved in the sports activities are seen with much improved physical and mental health as well as concentration in education. That’s why; it has been made compulsory as a part of the education in all the schools by the government. Boys and girls both are interested in this field and participate actively to win competitions at national and international level as well as make their career. Boys and girls practice running, swimming, jumping, discuss throw, javelin throw, etc at home, training center or school according to their interest.

Games and sports are indoor and outdoor activities involve both physical and mental effort. It involves in the overall growth and development of the children. People who do not involve in the physical activities can be weak physically and mentally, even they easily get diseases in early age. They are generally advised by the doctors to follow healthy life style and must add yoga, exercises, games, sports, etc to be fit and healthy. Parents should motivate their kids to involve in the indoor or outdoor games. Indoor games are like chess, carrom board, badminton, ludo, cards games, table tennis, etc. Outdoor games are like cricket, football, tennis, kabaddi, race, high or low jump, etc. Games and sports are very interesting and entertaining for the people who are interested. It is very necessary for the busy people (going to office, business, etc) to involve in some type of physical activities and then sports are best. They generally spend most of the day time by sitting or standing and night time by sleeping, thus they completely lack physical activities. Playing sports freshen the whole body including mind as well as it releases toxins from the whole body in the form of sweat. It reduces the aging in early age and keeps youthful for long time. Now-a-days, most of the people are suffering from overweight, obese, obesity and many other health issues only because of their sedentary lifestyle and completely lack of physical activities. Lots of work pressure but no sports or games, actually decreases our body and mind capacity. So, we should know the importance and value of games and sports and include at least one of them in our life.
